Manila

Intramuros, Manila

Historic Intramuros Tour with Food Crawl

This is a cultural and historical experience in Manila’s famous walled city, Intramuros. Visitors explore centuries-old landmarks that showcase Spanish-era architecture and learn about the city’s rich history. The food crawl adds a culinary element, offering a chance to taste traditional Filipino dishes from local restaurants.

Cebu

Alegria Town, Cebu Province

Kawasan Falls Adventure

A nature-filled tour in Cebu that combines marine life and waterfalls. Guests get to swim or snorkel alongside gentle whale sharks in Oslob, followed by a visit to Kawasan Falls, known for its turquoise waters and serene natural beauty.

Davao

Mt. Teril, Kapatagan, Digos City, Davao del Sur

Mt. Apo Climbing Experience

This is a trekking adventure to Mt. Apo, the highest mountain in the Philippines. It’s a physically challenging activity that rewards climbers with stunning views, diverse ecosystems, and a sense of accomplishment upon reaching the summit.
